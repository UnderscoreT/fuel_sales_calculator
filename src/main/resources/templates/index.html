How it works?

This application helps fuel attendants working with up to 6 nozzles calculate day-end sales. List of use cases:

  • Determine total cash sales
  • Get total litres sold
  • Calculate undeposited cash
  • Generate a breakdown of the cash in hand

Ensure starting/closing readings and product prices are entered correctly!

3 Easy Steps

01

Register Your Account

Create your account and login.

02

Log Your Sales Data

Enter opening and closing meter readings for each nozzle and log litres sold via coupons or card.

03

Get Your Totals

View your auto-calculated total sales and download a PDF report in one click.
