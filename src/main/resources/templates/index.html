This application can be used to help a fuel attendant quickly calculate the total number of litres they have sold using a pump with at most 2 nozzles. Listed below are other things that this web application can help an attendant do:

  • determine how much total cash sales they have done
  • get a summary of total litres sold
  • quickly calculate how much cash in hand they need to submit which was not dropped
