How it works?

This application can be used to help a fuel attendant using a pump with at most 6 nozzles calculate day end sales. Listed below are the overall capabilities that this web application enables attendants work out

  • determine how much total cash sales they have done
  • get a summary of total litres sold
  • quickly calculate how much cash in hand they need to submit which was not dropped
  • quickly make a breakdown of the cash in hand

Make sure your input for starting and closing pump meter readings and product price are correct!
