Oil-Change Basics: A Quick Guide for Attendants

Last updated: August 10, 2025 • 6–8 min read

Customers often ask about oil: which type, how often, and what the numbers mean. This guide helps you give accurate, simple answers.

Viscosity in Plain Language

  • Example: 5W-30—flows well cold (5W) and protects at hot engine temps (30).
  • Recommend checking the owner’s manual or cap label.

Change Intervals

  • Modern cars: typically 8,000–15,000 km depending on oil type and driving conditions.
  • Encourage customers to follow service schedule indicators on dash.

Top-Up vs. Full Change

  • Low oil? A top-up can be safe temporarily.
  • Dirty oil or overdue? Recommend a full service at a qualified workshop.

Forecourt Safety

  • Engine off during checks, avoid spills, keep rags away from heat.
  • Dispose of oily materials properly—follow station policy.

Help customers with clean info; leave the mechanical work to qualified techs. You keep everyone safe and informed.
