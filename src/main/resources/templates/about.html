About SizaFuel

Welcome to SizaFuel — your reliable web-based tool designed to make fuel station operations smoother and faster. Our application is built specifically for fuel attendants working with up to six nozzles, helping you calculate end-of-shift sales in minutes. It also provides access to attendants training guides.

I’m Obey Blessing Sibanda, a service attendant at DA Motors in Kuwadzana and the creator of SizaFuel. I built this tool after experiencing firsthand how long and error-prone manual pump meter calculations can be after a busy day. With SizaFuel, my mission is to save you time, reduce mistakes, and help you close your shift with confidence.

What You Can Do with SizaFuel

  • Instantly calculate total cash sales from multiple pumps
  • Get accurate summaries of total litres sold
  • Determine undropped cash to submit
  • Make quick, accurate cash breakdowns for banking

We believe in providing practical, easy-to-use tools that actually make your workday better. This platform is free to use and will keep evolving with feedback from real attendants.
