Privacy Policy for SizaFuel

Effective date: August 10, 2025

Welcome to SizaFuel (https://sizafuel.xyz). We respect your privacy. This Policy explains what we collect, why we collect it, how we use it, and the choices you have.

1) Who we are

SizaFuel provides tools for fuel sales calculations and related utilities (“Service”). Contact: support@sizafuel.xyz.

2) What we collect

  • App data you provide: fuel quantities, prices, sales totals, timestamps and other entries you choose to save, not for selling this data, but to let the user simply know how much they sold.
  • Device & access logs: IP address, approximate location inferred from IP (e.g., country/region or city), Internet Service Provider (ISP), browser and device information, date/time of access, pages visited, and referrer URLs.
  • Support contact: email address and message content when you email us.

We do not collect precise (GPS/Wi-Fi/cell-tower) location data and we do not collect payment card numbers or identity documents.

3) Why we use your information

  • Provide and maintain the Service, including saving your sales data on request.
  • Analytics to understand usage (e.g., traffic by country/region), improve features, and fix issues.
  • Security and fraud/abuse detection (e.g., blocking malicious traffic).
  • With your consent, personalize/measure ads and run analytics via trusted partners such as Google.

4) Legal bases (where applicable)

  • Legitimate interests: basic logging, security, and essential analytics.
  • Consent: non-essential cookies/SDKs, ad personalization, and advanced analytics. You can withdraw consent at any time.

5) Cookies, local storage & consent

We may use cookies, local storage, or similar technologies for essential functionality, analytics, and advertising. In regions where required (e.g., EEA/UK), we will ask for your consent before setting non-essential cookies or processing data for ad personalization. You can change your choice any time using the “Manage Privacy” control below.

6) Third-party services (Google)

We may use Google services such as Google Analytics and Google AdSense. These services may process your IP address, device information, and inferred approximate location to provide analytics, fight fraud/abuse, and (if you consent) deliver and measure personalized advertising. Data is transmitted over encrypted channels (HTTPS). Learn more: How Google uses data from sites or apps that use our services.

7) Sharing

We do not sell your personal information. We share data only with service providers who process it on our behalf for the purposes described above (e.g., hosting, analytics, advertising), under appropriate safeguards and confidentiality obligations.

8) Retention

Log data is retained for a limited period necessary for analytics and security, then deleted or aggregated. User-saved app data is kept until you delete it or request deletion.

9) Security

We use administrative, technical, and organizational measures to protect data; however, no method of transmission or storage is 100% secure.

10) Your rights & choices

  • Access, correct, or delete your data.
  • Withdraw consent where processing is based on consent.
  • Object to or restrict certain processing (where applicable).
  • “Do Not Sell or Share My Personal Information” (US-CA): we do not sell personal information; if you believe otherwise, contact us and we will honor your request.

Contact: support@sizafuel.xyz

The Service is not directed to children under 13. We do not knowingly collect data from children.

12) International transfers

Your data may be processed in countries other than your own, with protections consistent with this Policy and applicable law.

13) Changes

We may update this Policy from time to time. The latest version is posted at /privacy. Material changes will be highlighted.
