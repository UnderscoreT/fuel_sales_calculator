Calculating your pump sales (End-of-Day)

Last updated: August 10, 2025 • 7–9 min read

The 3-Block Workflow

  1. Readings: Capture start/end per nozzle (Blend A/B, Diesel A/B, Unleaded A/B).
  2. Non-cash litres: Coupon and card litres per product.
  3. Cash: Cash in hand + cash dropped = cash sales.

Using SizaFuel to Reconcile

  1. Enter start/end readings in the SizaFuel calculator.
  2. Add coupon/card litres and prices per litre.
  3. Get total litres, sales, and cash to submit—download/print your summary.

Common Errors & Fixes

  • Reversed readings: If end < start, re-check closing entry.
  • Wrong price per litre: Keep a price board photo for reference.
  • Missing coupon litres: Cross-check slips with pump totals.

Cash Breakdown = Faster Banking

Open Cash Breakdown Maker to total notes/coins by denomination. Attach to your sales report for clean handover.
