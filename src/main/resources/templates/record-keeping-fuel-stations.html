How to use sizafuel calculator (End of Day)

Operations • 7–9 min read • Updated 10 Aug 2025

TL;DR: Capture start/close readings, coupon/card litres, prices, and cash dropped — then reconcile. Use SizaFuel to speed it up and reduce mistakes.

EOD Checklist (Fast)

  • Start & end meter readings for each nozzle (A/B per product)
  • Price per litre for each product (Blend, Diesel, Unleaded)
  • Coupon litres and card litres by product
  • Cash dropped and any cash in hand

Calculate Totals

Use the calculator to compute litres sold per nozzle and per product. The app sums coupon/card litres and cash sales, then gives you final totals and a printable summary.

Reconciliation

  • Match cash sales against cash dropped + cash in hand
  • Confirm coupon/card litres vs receipts or system reports
  • Note anomalies (e.g., price entry errors, reading mis-keys)

Export & Handover

Download the PDF or summary and attach it to your shift file. Hand over a clean set of numbers for audit and the next shift lead.
